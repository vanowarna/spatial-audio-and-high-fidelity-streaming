ABX Listening Test

Spatial Audio & High-Fidelity Streaming

This test evaluates whether you can distinguish between original and compressed audio. You will hear three clips per trial: A (reference), B (alternate), and X (unknown). Your task is to decide if X = A or X = B.

Requirements: Use headphones in a quiet environment. The test takes approximately 10-15 minutes.
